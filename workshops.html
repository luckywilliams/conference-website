Job Search Track

Brianna Burrows - Nailing the Job Through Networking

Ever wonder how people get their dream job through networking and referrals? Come learn at this interactive workshop where you’ll learn how to find networking events, how to engage with strangers at networking events, and networking in the digital age through social media. Most importantly, you’ll learn the number one skill that prevents derailing your networking efforts!

Learn key insights and features that will help you attract potential employers, find that dream job and build an amazing professional brand.

Sunny Lee - Technical Recruiting Top Fives

LaQuita Hester - Build the Resume That Gets You Into the Door

LaQuita Hester - Negotiate Your Job Offer

LaQuita is reruiting manager at Avvo.

You did it - you got the job offer! Now what? Learn how to dissect your job offer and understand which parts are the most negotiable, how to best approach your negotiation.

Violetta Baeva - Technical Interview Strategies: Do's and Dont's

Violetta is a Software Engineer with 10 years of experience in back-end development, currently working at Apple. She is heavily involved in the hiring process, has interviewed over 200 engineers and was interviewed at many big tech companies.

Want to know how to perform better during interviews and get the jobs you want? Violetta will share her strategies for passing technical interviews. She will cover topics such as what to study well before an interview, mock interview setups and how to measure your progress, how to avoid candidates' most common mistakes during interviews, and how to drive an interview and make it hard for interviewers to reject you.

Career Development & Leadership Track

Executive leadership coach and career consultant Laura Close sees a select portfolio of professionals and entrepreneurs open to the transformative work of leadership development. Seattle clients book private sessions in her Pioneer Square office and remote clients see her live on Skype. Laura’s first book is due out in 2019. Learn more at LauraClose.com.

Join Seattle based executive leadership coach Laura Close to learn about power plateaus. Laura says: "You've done a great job building your career this far, in order for a breakthrough, you need fresh thinking." Learn the techniques and strategies for advancement that work for her clients.

Michele McCarthy and Tracey Assalone - Make It Happen: An Introduction to The Core Protocols

Michele is the creator of The Core Protocols and Owner of McCarthy Technologies.

Tracey is a formally trained relationship coach and clinical documentation specialist at Seattle Genetics.

Agile. Scrum. Values. Shared Vision. Diversity. There are so many ideas out there that sound wonderful but when it comes to actually making a change… things seem to go slowly or not change at all. The Core Protocols are the HOW for when you learn about a great idea. If you love the idea of Diversity, for example, but have difficulty getting it implemented, The Core Protocols will show you how to implement that idea. In this 3 hour workshop we’ll quickly get to the heart of The Core Protocols and you will learn how to make a big difference in your work and life right now. For more information checkout The McCarthy Show.

Janet Andrews - Feedback: Dealing with The Good, The Bad, and The Ugly

Janet has over 20 years of experience as a coach, consultant and business leader with emphasis on organizational strategy and change, and leadership development. She believes that high performing organizations and impacts start with individual leadership, no matter what role/level a person is within an organization. Janet helps leaders transition into new roles, maximize their opportunity, and build more resilient teams and programs. Learn more at Consociata.

If the idea of providing (or receiving) constructive feedback doesn't make you feel warm and fuzzy, you aren't alone. As social creatures, we like to be in harmony with others around us. However, our innovation and growth as individuals, and as teammates, are predicated on us taking in new perspectives - some of which may be counter to our existing beliefs. During this workshop, Janet will share with us the ins and outs of the feedback process that will help us be better prepared for these inevitable, everyday exchanges that we often find difficult and awkward. We'll learn to appreciate our human wiring towards feedback and conflict, notice our personal triggers, and understand how to apply empathetic listening as giver and receiver.

Nancy Jensen - Swinging Back and Forth to Work

Nancy is CEO and Co-Founder of The Swing Shift, the destination for women in career transition. She’s been featured in The Huffington Post, at Seattle’s infamous F-Bomb Breakfast Club and the Female Founders Alliance Champion Awards. The Swing Shift is on its 5th cohort, and a semi-finalist at Social Venture Partners 2017 Fast Pitch competition. Nancy believes that women shouldn’t be penalized for taking care of their families, is committed to getting women into the workforce on their own terms, and dedicated to 100% pay parity.

Are you looking to transition back into, or away from, a traditional workforce role? As many as 60% of women are looking to shift roles according to a 2017 Harris study, and as we know 43% of women take a break to raise their families. Let's talk about the current situation, what's needed for women who are looking to step back from full time and part time work, as well as women who are looking to step back in after a break. What resources do you need to make this switch, what are the barriers that you can expect in each case, and how can you overcome them?
